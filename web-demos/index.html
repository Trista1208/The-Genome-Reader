Genome Firewall — design explorations

Three directions for the demo site. Same content, different souls. Click through, view them full-screen, and pick a spine to refine.

A — Dala-inspired

Void Constellation

Pure black, electric-iris violet, amber sparks. Triangle particles form a rotating DNA helix — knowledge as a constellation. Sculptural 96px headlines, weight-200 body.
B — Auros-inspired

Abyssal Orb

Deep-teal terminal water, a bioluminescent particle sphere, aurora-gradient CTA, phosphor-pink statistics. Instrument-panel calm with cinematic depth.
C — Original direction

Biosecurity Terminal

The firewall made literal: a live ACGT sequence stream hits a violet gate — resistance markers burn out, clean bases pass. Monospace, scanlines, terminal modules.
D — Merged

The Full Stack

All three fused: Dala's void + triangle helix emits an ACGT stream that flows through the violet gate, over Auros bioluminescent dust. Aurora CTA, phosphor stats.
G — Hubtown × 0sec

HUD Monument

Hubtown's colossal Grotesk on 0sec's warm charcoal: ASCII shader field renders the helix as a density ramp, red = FAIL only, VT323 readouts, HUD corner brackets.
F — Hubtown-inspired

Monument

Colossal stacked statements on midnight navy #020a19, ice-blue type at 170px, weight-contrast headlines, an ACGT marquee ticker, verdicts told by weight not color.
H — Dala × 0sec

Blackwall

Dala's particle constellation living a full story: DNA helix → neural net → glitching crimson firewall slab. Warm charcoal, paper, signal red. Cyberpunk-grade.
I — Igloo-grade

Immersive World

Locked viewport, first-person travel: fly through the DNA helix tunnel, cross the neural lattice, arrive at the Blackwall. Real WebGL (Three.js), story beats, report at the end.
J — Immersive v2

Blackwall Gate

Solid-ribbon DNA that unravels into the lattice; the epic Blackwall monolith splits open as you arrive — you glide into the calm chamber where the report lives.
K — Immersive v2

Central Core

Same journey, different god: a pulsing wireframe heart with orbit rings and a point shell that expands around you as you fly into it. Report waits inside.
L — Strict wireframe

Wireframe Weave

Lines only — no particles, no sprites, no glitch. Wireframe DNA with hoops and rungs, and a spaceship camera that weaves around the bridges with gentle banking.
