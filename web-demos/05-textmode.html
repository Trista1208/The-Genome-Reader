system.modules

Three modules. One defensive layer.

strictly protective by construction — predicts and explains resistance that already exists. never designs, modifies, or suggests changes to an organism.

./01_genome_reader
AMRF+

genome reader

reconstructed FASTA annotated with NCBI AMRFinderPlus — known resistance genes and point mutations mapped into a documented, repeatable feature space.

./02_predictor
LOGREG

predictor

one calibrated model per antibiotic, trained on laboratory-measured outcomes only — de-duplicated by genetic similarity. it learns resistance, not look-alikes.

./03_decision_report
NO-CALL

decision report

every drug: a verdict, calibrated confidence, evidence category. weak or unfamiliar evidence returns a no-call — a strength, not a failure.
