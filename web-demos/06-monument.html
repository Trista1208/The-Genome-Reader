01  Genome Reader

The answer
is already
written down.

A reconstructed FASTA is annotated with NCBI AMRFinderPlus — known resistance genes and point mutations mapped into a documented, repeatable feature space. From DNA to features, no magic.

02  Predictor

One model.
One drug.
No shortcuts.

One calibrated model per antibiotic, trained only on laboratory-measured outcomes, de-duplicated by genetic similarity — near-identical genomes never leak across splits. It learns resistance, not look-alikes.

03  Decision Report

We say
no-call
on purpose.

Every drug gets a verdict, a calibrated confidence, and an evidence category — known marker, statistical association, or no signal. Forcing yes/no creates false confidence. Weak evidence returns a no-call. That is a strength.
